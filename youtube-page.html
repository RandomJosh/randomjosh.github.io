Youtube

This image was made to represent the YouTube side of me. I added the glow and bevel to the originally 2D flat image, this makes it look like an actual physical object which I am holding and also draws most of the focus to the well known logo. I did also add the deep blue eyes and vibrance just to make the entire image pop and stand out.

I got started on YouTube as a joke with my friends. We used to watch loads of YouTubers making funny videos such as gameplays and decided that I should be the one to get the channel. Back then little did I know that I would continue this seriously or even reach 200 subscribers!

Thank you to everyone of my lovely Randomers who have come on my journey with me, new or old!
